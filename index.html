Work

Although I grew up investing a lot of my time indoors on electronics, I also spent a big portion of my time outside. I grew up playing soccer and I continued to play until I left for college. I now attribute my ability to work with a team and in social environments to this opportunity. Among my list of outdoor activities is working with kids. Alongside my time spent outside, I spent a lot of time honing my programming skills through video tutorials and friends who shared common interests. Without any professional education on topics like these, most of my projects were pretty simple. Now that I have the opportunity to learn some CS in college, I am excited to see where my ideas take me.
